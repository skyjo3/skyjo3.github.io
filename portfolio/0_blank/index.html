ABSOLUT VODKA

by Amy Yo Sue Chen, Huan He

Absolut Vodka has its character, not only for business concept but for visual design. However, while persisting in its bottle shape and the written characters on the surface, it varies with the color combination and association of ideas in an absolutely crazy way. Changing the normal image card into the exterior of the packaging, the written characters reveal generously out- side the corrugated paperwork. Also, the interior anima- tion can be seen at the same time unfolding the image card.
